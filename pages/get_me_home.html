Get Me Home & Favourites

"Get Me Home" is a service based on, and similar to, the "Citymapper" application. "Get Me Home" will take the process one step further by seamlessly ordering tickets, booking taxis, or planning walking routes to get you home without having to reach for your wallet.

This page will also serve users with their favourite tickets and services. These include their most purchased food items or beverages and their regular tickets, for example season tickets.
